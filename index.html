Last Updated: November 2, 2025

Quick Summary: This application collects publicly available data from Facebook Pages for competitive analysis and market research purposes. We do not collect personal information from individual users.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Competitive Analysis Tool ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Facebook application. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects only publicly available information from Facebook Pages, including:

  • Page Information: Page name, username, category, follower count, and basic page metadata
  • Public Posts: Post content, publication dates, post types, and links
  • Engagement Metrics: Number of reactions, comments, and shares on public posts
  • Public Page Details: About section, website links, and other publicly displayed information

Important: We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information from individual Facebook users
  • Private messages or non-public content
  • Email addresses or contact information of page followers
  • Any information from user profiles beyond page administrators who authorize our app

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Competitive Analysis: Analyzing and comparing social media performance across multiple brands or organizations
  • Market Research: Understanding industry trends, content strategies, and audience engagement patterns
  • Business Intelligence: Generating reports and insights for strategic decision-making
  • Performance Benchmarking: Measuring and comparing social media metrics across competitors
